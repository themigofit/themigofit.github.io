FAQ

1. What types of recipes are included in the cookbook?
– All sorts, from pizzas and pastas to delicious desserts.
2. Are the recipes suitable for different dietary preferences and restrictions?

– Yes, we have a small section for vegan recipes as well as recipes for meat, chicken, and seafood.

3. Do you provide nutritional information for each recipe?

– Of course, and not only that, almost every recipe has a different version of calories like low, moderate, and high.

4. Is the cookbook available in both digital and print formats?

– No, only digital copies are available.

5. Are the recipes categorized by meal type (e.g., breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks)?

– Yes, but in sections like chicken proteins, meat proteins, seafood proteins, vegan proteins, how to cook essential carbs, desserts, and healthy sauces.

6. Are there high-protein or low-carb recipe options included?
  • Yes, of course.
7. What types of coaching programs do you offer?*

– We offer nutrition and workout plans for different periods, including 6-month, 3-month, and monthly plans. You can select the best suitable package on our website.

8. How do I sign up for your online coaching programs?

– By selecting the best suitable package on our website. After making the purchase, we will send you a questionnaire to design the best- suited plans for you.

9. What is included in the personalized fitness and nutritional plans?

 – Our plans include diet plans with calculated calories, proteins, carbs, and fats, a workout plan suitable to your daily routines, a food replacements file, and recommendations for supplements and essential vitamins if needed.

10. What kind of results can I expect from your coaching programs ?

– Whether your goal is bulking or cutting, we can help you achieve it through our tailored plans and support.

11. How do you provide progress tracking and accountability?

– We offer weekly progress pictures and Inbody tests when needed to track your progress and keep you accountable.

12. How do you tailor your coaching to individual needs and goals?

 -We design our programs based on the information you provide in the questionnaire we send you before starting the program.
